ONYXBONE

Episode 01 – "The Broadcast Shell"

TEASER

FADE IN:
EXT. ESTONIA – TALLINN HOSPITAL – NIGHT
Moonlight fractures against a windowpane. Wind scatters paper across an empty corridor. The hum of failing fluorescent tubes syncs with an irregular heart monitor beep.
A SHADOWED FIGURE stumbles through frame. Their bones cast shapes inside their skin—not reflections, but something else. The camera jerks. Interference.
A subtitle flickers:
TRIGRAM. BONE. MIRROR. INITIATE.
INT. UK – SKY NEWS STUDIO – NIGHT
A news presenter blinks slowly, visibly disturbed, watching the same footage loop behind them. Audio interference jitters their microphone. A second hum enters—lower, beneath human hearing. But the presenter pauses.
PRESENTER (V.O.)
Tonight, we share what cannot be unshared. Viewers are advised that this material... may alter perception.
Across Europe, homes flicker with the image. In one, a young woman—NAILA—stares at the screen, then her own arm. Her fingers twitch. Her bones begin to... lean.
INT. PUB – THE GOLDEN CONDOR, EAST LONDON – SAME TIME
The crowd watches in silence. Pint glasses tremble slightly. DEAN looks to JAY, who is laughing. But JAY'S molars glint metallic. Something is written on them.
NAILA
Did... you feel that?
JAY
It’s just the algorithm. Or maybe it’s your third eye uploading. Whatever, babe.
A BOOM vibrates the televisions. White flash. Briefly, everyone’s bones show through their skin in sharp silhouette. Then normalcy.
INT. UNKNOWN LOCATION – SURVEILLANCE ROOM
Monitors track NAILA, DEAN, JAY, and dozens more. A map updates in real time. A gloved hand taps on her femur scan.
OPERATIVE (O.S.)
Subject Naila. Justified: Carrier candidate. Initiate echo-seeding.
EXT. LONDON SKYLINE – NIGHT
Faint, across the clouds: a glyph is blooming. The city does not see it. But the BONE knows.
FADE OUT.
END OF TEASER

ACT ONE

FADE IN:
INT. THE GOLDEN CONDOR PUB – NIGHT
NAILA sits in a cracked-leather booth, fingers around a pint glass. Her eyes reflect the muted TV. On screen: looping skeletal footage.
DEAN
It's not real. Deepfake theatre.
AADAM
My cousin’s in Narva. She says hospitals are closed. They’re calling it tuberculosis but... it hums. Under your skin.
NAILA
I think I felt it. Last night. My wrist twitched like it was... answering something.
INT. NHS CLINIC – EXAMINATION ROOM – FLASHBACK
A flickering light. Naila in a hospital gown. A GP stares at an X-ray.
GP
Probably artefact contamination. Button on your coat maybe. Try a different clinic.
INT. GCHQ MONITORING HUB – NIGHT
Glyphs appear on biometric overlays. Operators whisper to each other.
OPERATIVE
This isn’t behavioural drift. These glyphs are anatomical. Bone-encoded.
SUPERVISOR
Tag all occurrences. Pull prior imagery. Find anyone who saw that footage.
INT. SKY NEWS STUDIO – CONTROL ROOM – NIGHT
JULIE TURNER stares at an incoming call sheet. Her assistant shakes his head.
JULIE
No one’s answering at Tallinn. Run the 16-second clip on loop. Mask the glyph.
ASSISTANT
What if they already saw it?
INT. THE GOLDEN CONDOR – NIGHT
A low hum vibrates the metal bar taps. Naila looks up. Jay is laughing. Dean is texting.
NAILA
I’m going home.
JAY
What for? The footage doesn’t bite.
Naila stands. Her knees almost buckle. She clutches her thigh.
NAILA (V.O.)
It was awake. Inside the bone.
INT. NAILA’S FLAT – BATHROOM – LATER
Naila breathes hard. Her notebook is spread on the sink. Diagrams. Glyphs. A phrase scrawled repeatedly:
TRIGRAM. BONE. MIRROR. INITIATE.
INT. COBR BRIEFING ROOM – NIGHT
Military. NHS. GCHQ. Prime Minister. Red folders everywhere. A projected image: a skeletal system with pulsing glyphs.
PM
Can it be reversed?
MOD INTELLIGENCE
No. We’re past mitigation. It's cognitive. Semantic. Biological.
PM
Then what’s the strategy?
GCHQ LIAISON
Counter-naming. Or silence.
FADE OUT.
END OF ACT ONE

ACT TWO

FADE IN:
INT. NHS A&E – NIGHT
The waiting room buzzes with fluorescent agony. Screens glitch. Patients clutch at joints. Bone reports are flooding in.
TRIAGE NURSE
Three more SKE-145s. That makes twenty today.
DOCTOR
Log it under 'bone resonance'. Flag if they start naming.
A teenager whispers “fibula” into his hand. Something shifts beneath his skin.
INT. SKY NEWS STUDIO – 22:03
JULIE watches the silent footage loop. Subtitles flash: "UNKNOWN SKELETAL RESPONSE." Her earpiece hisses with static.
PRESENTER (V.O.)
...glyph patterns first seen in hospital footage now confirmed across five cities. Government remains silent.
JULIE
Delay the next segment. I want more on the hum.
INT. NAILA’S FLAT – LIVING ROOM – SAME NIGHT
NAILA sketches symbols into her notebook. Glyphs. Radiant curves. Her phone buzzes.
JAY (TEXT)
Are you okay? Are you watching this?
NAILA (V.O.)
April 3rd. 23:11. My fingers feel like they're echoing. I heard someone say 'mandible' in my sleep. I think it was me.
INT. DARK ROOM – GOVERNMENT NODE – UNDISCLOSED
A projected bone lattice rotates slowly. Surveillance footage is overlaid. Names appear beside body parts.
DATA TECHNICIAN
Every flagged X-ray is aligning. They're forming phrases. The bones are speaking.
OPERATIONS OFFICER
Suppress the footage. Reclassify the scans. Anyone with glyph emergence gets moved to Tier 3.
INT. JAY’S BEDROOM – SAME TIME
JAY lays awake, scrolling through conspiracy threads. Posts flash by: “They’re naming bones.” “My dog said ulna.”
JAY
(laughs to himself) It’s all just panic theatre.
He rubs his jaw. Winces.
INT. HOSPITAL LAB – NIGHT
A TOOTH floats in solution. Magnified: its inner rings pulse with light. A faint hum. A lab tech’s nose bleeds.
LAB TECHNICIAN
It’s reacting to audio. Every time someone says “humerus,” the sample twitches.
INT. NAILA’S FLAT – BATHROOM – LATER
NAILA brushes her teeth. Stops. Opens her mouth. The mirror fogs. In condensation, a word appears:
Sternum
NAILA
(staring) No.
She backs away. Her shoulder twitches. She drops the toothbrush.
INT. BBC – BROADCAST ARCHIVE NODE – SIMULTANEOUS
An engineer replays the Tallinn clip frame by frame. At frame 187, a glyph pulses in the clavicle.
ENGINEER
They embedded it. The signal. The glyph's a trigger. It’s not visual—it’s marrow deep.
INT. NAILA’S BEDROOM – NIGHT
She lies still. Lights off. Bones humming beneath skin.
NAILA (V.O.)
If I write it, it won’t be mine anymore. But if I don’t, it grows louder.
Her notebook is open beside her. It reads:
Radius: Named. Justified: Vector of Will
FADE OUT.
END OF ACT TWO

ACT THREE

FADE IN:
INT. NAILA’S BEDROOM – EARLY MORNING
Dawn spills over stacked notebooks. Naila has not slept. The glyph she saw in condensation appears now in ink—without her knowing she wrote it.
NAILA (V.O.)
I said nothing. But my ulna answered.
INT. SCHOOL CORRIDOR – DAY
Children move in eerie silence. One child stands at a vending machine, hand pressed against the glass.
CHILD
Scapula. Justified: hinge of longing.
His hand does not come away. Teachers scream.
INT. HOSPITAL – OBSERVATION UNIT – SAME TIME
A dozen patients hum in synch. Their bones emit faint light under the skin. Nurses observe behind mirrored glass.
NURSE
They’re not sedated. They’re synchronised.
SURGEON
Do not name any bones aloud. We’ve already lost the night staff.
INT. COBR – EMERGENCY COMMS – DAY
COBR staff now work in silence. Post-it notes forbid anatomical references. A language containment specialist speaks:
LINGUIST
Words are encoding shape. We need a counter-semantics grid. Issue the Justification Ban now.
INT. DEAN’S FLAT – DAY
DEAN plays back Naila’s voicemail. Her voice is distorted. It folds in on itself—speaking glyphs.
...radius... justified... justified... name it... accept...
INT. SOCIAL MEDIA HUB – SIMULTANEOUS
Meme pages are saturated. Glyphs in filters. #NameYourBone trends. A livestream cuts to a young woman naming each vertebra.
INFLUENCER
Atlas. Justified: bearing the head. Axis. Justified: turn of destiny. C3. Justified:...
She begins to shake. Her voice fractures.
INT. NAILA’S FLAT – LIVING ROOM – NIGHT
Jay paces.
JAY
It’s your fault. You brought this here. You’ve been writing it. Whispering in your sleep.
NAILA
I only wrote what the radius said.
Jay recoils. The lights blink. A pulse of low-frequency hum spreads outward. All glass fractures in concentric glyphs.
INT. GLOBAL SECURITY NETWORK – TIER 4 ROOM – SAME TIME
Generals argue. Glyphic diagrams pulse behind them.
INTELLIGENCE CHIEF
It’s past control. Each utterance embeds. Every name justified becomes a command.
PRIME MINISTER (O.S.)
What do we do?
INTELLIGENCE CHIEF
We must silence language.
FADE OUT.
END OF ACT THREE

ACT FOUR

FADE IN:
INT. NAILA’S FLAT – NIGHT
Naila sits on the floor surrounded by open books and printouts. Her eyes are bloodshot. Her voice is low, rhythmic.
NAILA
Mandible. Justified: engine of prophecy. Sternum. Justified: hinge of breath.
Jay, in the kitchen, stares at her. His jaw clenches. A faint hum builds.
JAY
They’ll come for you, Nai. You’re not safe.
INT. LONDON UNDERGROUND – LATE NIGHT
A man on a packed tube train starts chanting bone names. Panic spreads. People claw at their skin. One woman vomits glyphs.
INT. GLOBAL MEDIA MONITORING CENTER – SAME TIME
Feeds flood with the glyph symbol. Memes overlay anatomy diagrams. Hashtags translate into bone-names.
DATA ANALYST
It’s recursive. It’s rewriting the net. Every post strengthens it.
INT. PRIVATE DEFENSE BUNKER – NIGHT
A NATO commander draws a glyph on a map. Behind him, a projected world model shows glowing bone clusters over every city.
COMMANDER
We tried to jam the signal. But it's not a signal. It's language. Spoken bone. We can’t unhear it.
INT. CLASSROOM – MORNING
A substitute teacher speaks carefully.
TEACHER
We won’t say the names today. Let’s focus on quiet drawing. No anatomy.
One child draws a glyph in perfect symmetry.
CHILD
It showed me.
INT. SKY NEWS – BACK OFFICE
Julie watches a slowed-down version of Naila’s interview two years ago. In the background—barely visible—a glyph pulses behind her shoulder.
JULIE
She’s been carrying it since before Tallinn.
INT. NAILA’S FLAT – NIGHT
Naila draws her hand across the wall. A bone map appears under the wallpaper. Glowing faintly.
NAILA
This flat is a vessel. I am a hinge. This episode ends with me.
INT. HOSPITAL – GLYPH CONTAINMENT WARD
Rooms fill with whisperers. Every wall is mirrored. Their bones reflect infinitely. Some faces become symbols.
EXT. SKYLINE – PRE-DAWN
The glyph from Tallinn now floats over London, barely visible to the untrained eye.
FADE OUT.
END OF ACT FOUR
