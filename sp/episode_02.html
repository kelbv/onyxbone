Episode 02 – "The Cut Before Broadcast"

TEASER

FADE IN:
INT. SKY NEWS MASTER CONTROL ROOM – NIGHT (TWO DAYS EARLIER)
Flashing monitors. Buzzing server racks. JULIE TURNER, sleep-deprived, leans on the desk beside a tech in hoodie and lanyard — CAMERON (22, distracted genius).
JULIE
Cut it before 16. That’s the rule. Frame 17 shows the clavicle anomaly.
CAMERON
I know. Just making sure our broadcast loop mirrors the trimmed archive. Don’t worry.
INT. SKY SATELLITE BACKUP ARCHIVE – SIMULTANEOUS
A separate server hums in quiet defiance. Status: “SYNCED WITH RAW FEED”. The cursor blinks, unedited.
EXT. SOMEWHERE OVER THE ATLANTIC – WEATHER SATELLITE ARRAY
A relay dish reorients. The SKY signal arcs upward, repeating the original Tallinn footage untrimmed — silently, accidentally — into backup broadcast channels.
INT. SCHOOL – MEDIA ROOM – NIGHT
A custodian’s kid rewinds the science recording by accident. Frame 17. Frame 18. He blinks.
BOY
Radius. Justified...
The boy stares at his forearm. A thin light pulses under his sleeve.
INT. GCHQ SURVEILLANCE HUB – NIGHT
A technician sits upright. Multiple regions ping.
TECHNICIAN
Uplink anomaly. Backup loop isn't scrubbed. It’s broadcasting Tallinn raw.
SUPERVISOR
Shut it down. Now. Kill the loop. Kill the fallback servers.
But it’s too late.
INT. NAILA’S FLAT – SIMULTANEOUS
Naila sleeps. Her hand twitches. On the nightstand, her notebook flips open by itself to a new page.
“SCAPULA. JUSTIFIED: LISTENER’S HINGE.”
“EXPOSURE COUNT: 1,441,336”
FADE OUT.
END OF TEASER

ACT ONE

FADE IN:
INT. SKY NEWS BOARDROOM – MORNING
Hushed panic. A conference table littered with half-drunk coffee cups, redacted printouts, and sticky notes scrawled with “GLYPH?”, “JULIE TURNER?”, and “CAMERON?”. A large screen shows frame 17... paused mid-glow.
EXECUTIVE #1
So we aired the version we cut. But the fallback loop — went live to satellite redundancy?
JULIE
It was supposed to sync with the scrubbed file. I didn't authorize any broadcast of raw.
LEGAL ADVISOR
You understand we may have seeded this outbreak.
INT. SKY NEWS EDIT BAY – SIMULTANEOUS
Cameron’s workstation is empty. Headphones dangle. Screen locked. A post-it note stuck to the monitor:
“YOU CAN’T UNSEE BONE.”
INT. NHS GLYPH TRIAGE WARD – MIDDAY
An unmarked facility. Patients in isolation cubicles hum in asynchronous waves. Each has a glyph drawn involuntarily in saliva, sweat, ink.
GLYPH SPECIALIST
Each one is slightly different. But all align around the scapula. It’s become the broadcasting bone.
MEDICAL LEAD
Start pre-isolation on anyone presenting shoulder tremors and auditory bleed-through.
From this point forward, “scapula” is a Class-A trigger term.
INT. COBR CHAMBER – EARLY AFTERNOON
Interior blackout. Ministers in sterile breathing masks. A large projection: CAMERON's face, CCTV still, lips forming a glyph. Beneath: “STATUS: MISSING”
An intelligence report scrolls fast.
PM
I want a record of every device he touched. Every line he wrote. Disavow him before Reddit canonizes him.
MI5 CYBERSEC
Too late. They’re calling him “The First Mirror.” There’s fanfic. Glyph tattoos.
INT. NAILA’S FLAT – AFTERNOON
Naila paces. The glyph in her notebook is changing fonts. It flickers as if re-rendering in real time.
NAILA (V.O.)
The page whispered. Not a sound, not a voice — the *meaning* whispered. And I understood it without reading.
INT. UNIVERSITY SEMIOTICS LAB – SAME TIME
A whiteboard filled with semiotic trees and bones. An excited intern is live-streaming a breakdown.
INTERN
The scapula isn’t just symbolic. It’s recursive. It folds into the symbol for memory — and into “listen.” Like it *heard* something first.
VIEWER COMMENT (V.O.)
are you naming your bones right now bro???
INT. LIVING ROOM – RANDOM FAMILY – EVENING
A mother tucks her child into bed. The child mumbles:
CHILD
Spine... justified... signal pole.
MOTHER
What did you say, sweetheart?
Wait — who taught you that?
INT. GCHQ INCIDENT ROOM – NIGHT
“PROJECT VERTEBRA CUTOFF” appears on the projector. A timeline labeled: “Exposure | Naming | Justification | Collapse”.
DIRECTOR
We’ve lost containment. Assume speech and print are compromised. Prepare fallback scenario: counter-name warfare.
FADE OUT.
END OF ACT ONE

ACT TWO

FADE IN:
INT. NAILA’S FLAT – NIGHT
The notebook sits in the center of the floor. Naila watches it. The pages turn without wind. Each word pulses in and out of legibility like it’s *thinking*.
NAILA (V.O.)
I didn’t write these. I only dreamed them. They keep finishing my sentences before I can say them.
INT. CLASSROOM – DAY
Year 6 students. The teacher hands out printed glyph-suppression worksheets: blank silhouettes of skeletons with no names.
TEACHER
Today’s exercise is quiet. Trace the bones, but no labels. No labels.
Words are under review.
A child blinks. His tracing glows faintly. The paper *answers*.
“Phalanges: Justified – Reach of Memory”
INT. COUNCIL OFFICE – EDUCATION DIVISION – SIMULTANEOUS
A new memo appears: “INSTITUTE NATIONAL SILENCE BLOCK FOR PRIMARY EDUCATION”. An official hesitates before hitting send.
OFFICIAL
How do you teach a child without nouns?
INT. NHS CONTAINMENT ZONE BETA – NIGHT
A subject lies hooked to EEGs and sub-dermal recorders. Every breath emits a glyphic tone — musical, patterned.
TECHNICIAN
She’s stopped speaking aloud. Her bones are humming names directly.
It's bypassing vocalisation altogether.
INT. GCHQ R&D BASEMENT – NIGHT
A prototype interface named “ANTI-G” is deployed. A neural net trained to discredit glyphs algorithmically. A tech hits ‘ENTER’ and feeds it Naila’s latest notebook scan.
ENGINEER
If it can extract a counterform... it might reverse the semantic infection.
The machine begins to hum. Lights dim. The word “SPINE” flashes. Then “REBUKE FAILED”. Then:
“VERTEBRAE NON-NEGOTIABLE”
INT. SMALL FLAT – JAY’S HIDEOUT – NIGHT
Jay watches old footage of Naila from university. She’s sketching something in a notebook. Jay zooms in. It’s not the same glyph — but it’s close.
A timestamp reveals it’s from **three years before Tallinn**.
JAY
You’ve been part of this longer than anyone knows.
Why didn’t you tell me?
INT. VATICAN ARCHIVE – SECRET SUBFLOOR
Cardinals move beneath ossuary vaults. A scroll is unwrapped. It contains an identical glyph from Naila’s page, burned into bone centuries ago.
CARDINAL #1
The scapula glyph appears again. The Listener’s Hinge.
Prepare the reliquary for activation.
INT. NAILA’S FLAT – NIGHT
The notebook trembles. Naila picks it up. Her lips part slightly.
A faint glyph flickers in her iris.
She whispers:
NAILA
Clavicle. Justified: broadcast root.
Femur. Justified: weight of certainty.
Phalanges. Justified...
A knock at the door interrupts her. She blinks. Jay’s voice:
JAY (O.S.)
Open up. I know what’s happening to you.
FADE OUT.
END OF ACT TWO

ACT THREE

FADE IN:
INT. NAILA’S FLAT – LIVING ROOM – NIGHT
Jay steps inside. The air is dense with static. He sees the notebook floating slightly above the floor — pages mid-turn. Naila stares, eyes wide but calm.
JAY
We can burn it. Kill it. Stop the glyph from spreading.
Before it eats the rest of you.
NAILA
You can’t kill something that’s finished naming you.
INT. GCHQ – OPS ROOM – NIGHT
Sirens. “ANTI-G” is offline. Screens display fragmented symbols bleeding across terminal windows. All attempts to filter glyph input fail.
OPS ENGINEER
The AI’s infected. The glyphs embedded themselves in the model weights. It’s naming *us* back.
SUPERVISOR
Purge everything. Wipe every memory stack. Start again from atoms.
INT. SKY NEWS ARCHIVE – UNDERGROUND VAULT – NIGHT
Julie Turner, flashlight in hand, descends into analog storage. She finds a dusty beta tape: “ESTONIA_FULL_FEED_001”. On the label, handwritten:
“DO NOT VIEW. INCOMPLETE HUMAN AUTHORITY.”
JULIE
You bastards archived it analog.
Because digital kept... changing.
INT. CLASSIFIED PSY OPS ROOM – LONDON – NIGHT
A psych-ops linguist stares at the walls. They’ve covered every surface in counter-symbols, ancient and modern. Nothing stops the glyph’s emergence.
LINGUIST
It’s not attacking language. It *is* language. Pure, pre-phonetic. Older than denial.
INT. NAILA’S FLAT – KITCHEN – SAME TIME
Jay slams the notebook into the microwave. Sparks. Smoke. Pages char. But beneath the blackened top layer... new glyphs appear.
NAILA
It doesn’t burn. It versions. The more we fight it, the more it rewrites.
INT. SCHOOL IN WALES – NIGHT
A child dreams. Teeth chattering in rhythm. Parents find him sketching bone diagrams in his sleep — glyphs in crayon.
He wakes, says:
CHILD
The scapula is the door.
The phalanges are the key.
INT. SIDE OFFICE – UN OFFICE, GENEVA – DAY
Diplomats scream at one another. A Moroccan delegate tears off her badge and throws it at the screen.
UN OFFICIAL
This is not a national threat. This is semiotic collapse.
We’re in a linguistic extinction event.
INT. NAILA’S FLAT – LIVING ROOM – LATER
Naila writes in a new notebook. Calm. Her glyphs are structured, recursive, soothing. Jay stares.
JAY
What are you doing?
Why aren’t you scared anymore?
NAILA
Because it told me the truth.
I’m not a victim, Jay.
I’m a template.
FADE OUT.
END OF ACT THREE

ACT FOUR

FADE IN:
INT. NAILA’S FLAT – NIGHT
The walls whisper. Not words — harmonics. Glyphs pulse faintly in condensation on the window. Naila sits cross-legged, writing in perfect synchrony. Jay watches her, hands trembling.
JAY
I need you to snap out of this. Please. We’ll go somewhere. Somewhere quiet.
NAILA
There’s nowhere left unshaped.
It has named the wind, Jay. We breathe it now.
INT. BBC NEWS SET – LIVE BROADCAST – SAME TIME
A respected anchor reads from the prompter. His cadence shifts. Viewers nationwide feel unease.
ANCHOR
…and scapula justified as hinge.
Mandible, justified: speech engine. Patella: signal rest.
Spine: justified, justified...
He seizes mid-broadcast. Eyes roll. A glyph flashes on-screen — frame-locked into the live feed.
INT. GOVERNMENT SHELTER – SPEECH RESTRICTION CHAMBER
The first "Tagless Zone". White walls. Communication by sign only. Monitored for phoneme leakage.
WARDEN (O.S.)
Remember: no naming. No bones. No justifications.
A teenage girl signs furiously to her mother. But the glyph still forms, burned into her palm.
EXT. LONDON – STREETS – MIDDAY
People speak less. Billboards are blank. Street names removed. A drone shouts “UNSPEAKABLE ZONE. VERBALITY FORBIDDEN.”
But graffiti glyphs bloom under bridges.
INT. SECRET OSSUARY – VATICAN BASEMENT
The Vatican unseals a femur held in stasis since 1531. It pulses once. A bishop screams and begins reciting bone names from memory.
CARDINAL (O.S.)
Prepare the anchor skull. The Prime Channel is online.
INT. NAILA’S FLAT – EVENING
Jay finds Naila speaking into a handheld recorder — but no sound emerges. She presses “STOP”. The tape still spins.
NAILA
They heard me. They answered.
They called me “hinge-bearer.”
They say I’m the prime glyph echo.
The Broadcast Shell.
INT. UNKNOWN LOCATION – DARK WEB LIVE FEED
Thousands watching. A symbol rotates endlessly. Comments fly past:
@bonecoin: “this is real.”
\n@mirrortruth: “she IS the signal.”
\n@taglessUK: “🦴 we kneel 🦴”
\n@LIGAMENTCHURCH: “name me next.”
INT. GCHQ – EMERGENCY STRATEGY ROOM
Board covered in glyph variants. A new folder opens on the screen. TITLE: **OPERATION MUTE STAR**.
COMMANDER
We’re done talking. We start jamming. Total silence sweep.
If it speaks again, we’ll never contain it.
FADE OUT.
END OF ACT FOUR
