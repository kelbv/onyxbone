TEASER
FADE IN:
EXT. TALLINN HOSPITAL - NIGHT
A handheld camera captures shaky footage of a hospital corridor. Fluorescent lights flicker erratically. A FIGURE shuffles into frame, movements jerky and unnatural. As they approach, their bones glow faintly through their skin, casting angular shadows. They speak in a distorted, multi-tonal voice.
FIGURE
(subtitled)
Trigram. Bone. Mirror. Initiate.
The camera trembles, pixelation surges across the screen, and the footage abruptly cuts to black.
FADE OUT.
END OF TEASER
ACT ONE
FADE IN:
INT. THE GOLDEN CONDOR PUB - NIGHT
The pub is alive with chatter and the clink of glasses. NAILA, early 30s, sharp-eyed but weary, sits at a booth with DEAN and JAY, both around her age. They’re mid-conversation, but Naila stares at the foam in her pint, lost in thought.
DEAN
(leaning in)
You alright? You’ve been quiet all night.
NAILA
Yeah, just… been having weird dreams lately.
JAY
What kind of dreams?
NAILA
Hard to explain. Like, I hear this humming sound, and my bones feel like they’re vibrating.
DEAN
(laughs)
Sounds like you need to lay off the late-night horror flicks.
NAILA
It’s not funny. It feels real.
The TV in the corner switches to a muted Sky News broadcast. Patrons turn to watch as captions flicker: “BREAKING - UNVERIFIED FOOTAGE LEAKED FROM TALLINN HOSPITAL - UK GOVT REFUSES COMMENT.” The screen replays the teaser footage—glowing bones, distorted words.
JAY
What the hell is that?
NAILA
(staring, hushed)
That’s… that’s what I saw in my dream.
DEAN
You’re kidding.
NAILA
No, I’m serious. This is exactly it.
Naila stands abruptly, pale and shaken, and heads for the door.
NAILA
I need some air.
EXT. THE GOLDEN CONDOR PUB - NIGHT
Naila steps into the cool night, breathing deeply. She glances at her wrist—a faint, glyph-like mark pulses under her skin. She whispers to herself.
NAILA
What is happening to me?
FADE OUT.
END OF ACT ONE
ACT TWO
FADE IN:
INT. NAILA'S APARTMENT - BEDROOM - NIGHT
Naila tosses in bed, restless. She bolts upright, grabs her phone, and searches “Tallinn hospital leak.” A conspiracy forum pops up with a post: “Vector Skeletion Protocol—biological override in bones. Classified.” Her screen glitches; a glyph flashes briefly.
A faint hum fills the room. Naila shivers, clutching her wrist where the mark glows stronger.
INT. HOSPITAL - LONDON - DAY
Naila sits in a sterile waiting room, anxious. She’s ushered into the doctor’s office.
INT. DOCTOR’S OFFICE - DAY
DOCTOR, 50s, examines Naila’s wrist. The glyph mark is vivid now.
DOCTOR
This is unusual. Looks like a tattoo, but you say it just appeared?
NAILA
Yes, overnight. And I’ve been feeling… vibrations in my bones. Hearing a hum.
DOCTOR
I’ll order tests. Rest for now.
Naila nods, unconvinced, and leaves.
EXT. LONDON STREET - DAY
Naila walks past a newsstand—headlines scream about Tallinn and global anomalies. She overhears a conversation at a café.
MAN
(to WOMAN)
Mind control experiment gone wrong, I reckon.
WOMAN
Or aliens. Who knows?
Naila shakes her head, but the doubt lingers.
FADE OUT.
END OF ACT TWO
ACT THREE
FADE IN:
INT. NAILA’S APARTMENT - LIVING ROOM - NIGHT
Naila pores over a laptop, reading a redacted leak about “Vector Skeletion Protocol.” Her phone rings—it’s Dean.
NAILA
(answering)
Hey, Dean.
DEAN (V.O.)
Naila, you okay? You bolted last night.
NAILA
I’m fine. Just figuring this out.
DEAN (V.O.)
That news was freaky. Come over if you need to talk.
NAILA
Maybe later. Thanks.
She hangs up. Her laptop flickers; the Tallinn footage plays unbidden. The FIGURE stares at the camera.
FIGURE
(subtitled)
You are not required to remember. Become the tool.
The screen cuts out. Naila recoils as the hum grows louder in her ears.
EXT. LONDON PARK - DAY
Naila walks, distracted, and spots a STREET ARTIST drawing glyphs on the pavement. One matches her wrist. She approaches.
NAILA
Excuse me, what are you drawing?
ARTIST
Ancient symbols. They speak to the soul.
NAILA
Where’d you learn them?
ARTIST
They come in dreams. I just draw what I see.
Naila backs away, chilled.
FADE OUT.
END OF ACT THREE
ACT FOUR
FADE IN:
INT. NAILA’S APARTMENT - BEDROOM - NIGHT
Naila sleeps fitfully. In her dream, she’s in a dark room with glowing skeletons chanting.
SKELETONS
Trigram. Bone. Mirror. Initiate.
Naila tries to run but can’t. The chanting intensifies; her bones vibrate. She wakes, sweating, and checks her wrist—the glyph glows brightly. In the bathroom mirror, her eyes flicker with faint light.
A VOICE echoes in her head, calm and commanding.
VOICE
You are chosen. Embrace the protocol.
Naila clutches her head, fighting it.
VOICE
Become the transmitter. Spread the signal.
Naila screams and collapses, the hum overwhelming.
CUT TO BLACK.
END OF EPISODE
