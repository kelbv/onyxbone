TEASER
FADE IN:
INT. NEWSROOM - NIGHT
Chaos reigns in a bustling newsroom. Reporters shout over each other, phones ring incessantly, and screens display the viral Tallinn hospital footage. A frazzled EDITOR slams a phone down.
EDITOR
Get me someone who can verify this! Now!
A young REPORTER rushes in, holding a tablet.
REPORTER
Sir, there’s a new leak. A whistleblower posted this on a darknet forum.
The tablet shows a cryptic message: "The hum is internal. They’re already inside us."
EDITOR
(grim)
This is going to be a long night.
FADE OUT.
END OF TEASER
ACT ONE
FADE IN:
INT. NAILA’S APARTMENT - LIVING ROOM - DAY
NAILA sits on her couch, staring at her glowing wrist glyph. DEAN and JAY stand nearby, concerned.
DEAN
You’re saying this thing just appeared overnight? And now you’re hearing voices?
NAILA
Not voices, exactly. It’s more like a hum, but it feels… meaningful.
JAY
(skeptical)
Maybe it’s stress. You’ve been through a lot lately.
NAILA
No, this is real. And it’s connected to that footage from Tallinn.
DEAN
The one with the glowing bones? You think that’s happening to you?
NAILA
I don’t know, but I need to find out.
Jay pulls out his laptop and starts typing.
JAY
Let me see what I can dig up. There’s got to be more info out there.
After a moment, Jay’s eyes widen.
JAY
Whoa, check this out. A darknet forum has a thread called "Vector Skeletion Protocol." It’s full of leaks and conspiracy theories.
NAILA
(leaning in)
What does it say?
JAY
It’s a classified project—something about a biological override protocol that turns bones into transmitters. There’s talk of glyphs, harmonics, and a global network.
DEAN
That sounds insane.
NAILA
But it matches what’s happening to me.
Suddenly, Naila winces, clutching her arm as the glyph pulses brighter.
NAILA
It’s spreading.
FADE OUT.
END OF ACT ONE
ACT TWO
FADE IN:
EXT. CITY STREETS - DAY
Newsstands display headlines about the Tallinn leak and reports of similar incidents worldwide. People whisper in hushed tones, some glancing nervously at their own limbs.
INT. NAILA’S APARTMENT - LIVING ROOM - DAY
Naila examines her arm—the glyph now extends to her elbow. She looks pale and shaken.
NAILA
I can hear it clearer now. It’s like a language, but not words.
DEAN
Maybe we should take you to a hospital.
NAILA
No, they won’t understand. I need to find out what this is.
Jay, still on his laptop, finds a new post.
JAY
There’s a user here claiming to be a whistleblower. They say the protocol was developed by a shadow government agency.
NAILA
Can we contact them?
JAY
I’ll try, but it’s risky. These forums are monitored.
As Jay types, the lights flicker. A low hum fills the room, and Naila’s glyph glows intensely.
NAILA
(whispering)
They know we’re looking.
INT. GOVERNMENT OFFICE - DAY
A stern OFFICIAL addresses a room of agents.
OFFICIAL
The leaks are contained for now, but we need to control the narrative. Deny everything. It’s a hoax, a viral stunt.
An AGENT raises a hand.
AGENT
What about the civilians showing symptoms? The glyphs?
OFFICIAL
Mass hysteria. Nothing more. Stick to the script.
FADE OUT.
END OF ACT TWO
ACT THREE
FADE IN:
INT. NAILA’S APARTMENT - LIVING ROOM - NIGHT
Naila and Jay pore over the laptop while Dean paces nervously.
JAY
I’ve got a response. The whistleblower wants to meet. They say they have proof.
NAILA
Where?
JAY
An abandoned warehouse in East London. Tonight.
DEAN
This sounds like a trap.
NAILA
I have to go. I need answers.
EXT. ABANDONED WAREHOUSE - NIGHT
Naila arrives at the eerie, derelict warehouse. Shadows loom, and the hum in her bones grows louder.
A FIGURE steps out from the darkness—hooded, face obscured.
FIGURE
You’re Naila?
NAILA
Yes. You’re the whistleblower?
FIGURE
Call me Echo. I worked on the project. It’s worse than you think.
Echo hands Naila a flash drive.
ECHO
This has everything—documents, videos, test results. But be careful. They’re watching.
Suddenly, headlights flood the warehouse. Black SUVs screech to a halt outside.
ECHO
Run!
Naila bolts, clutching the flash drive, as armed agents storm in.
FADE OUT.
END OF ACT THREE
ACT FOUR
FADE IN:
EXT. LONDON ALLEYWAY - NIGHT
Naila sprints through dark alleys, agents in pursuit. Her glyph pulses, guiding her turns.
She ducks into a narrow passage, losing them temporarily. Panting, she plugs the flash drive into her phone.
INT. NAILA’S PHONE SCREEN
Files open: classified documents, videos of experiments, and a map of glyph hotspots worldwide.
One video shows a scientist speaking.
SCIENTIST (ON VIDEO)
The Vector Skeletion Protocol is the next step in human evolution. A collective consciousness through bone resonance.
Naila’s eyes widen in horror.
NAILA
(to herself)
They’re turning us into a hive mind.
Footsteps approach. Naila hides the phone and runs again.
EXT. LONDON BRIDGE - NIGHT
Naila reaches the bridge, agents closing in. Her glyph now covers her entire arm, glowing brightly.
She climbs onto the railing, desperate. The hum crescendos, and she hears a voice in her head.
VOICE
Embrace the protocol. Become one with the network.
Naila hesitates, then jumps into the Thames as agents reach the bridge.
Underwater, her glyph illuminates the dark water, and she swims away, the hum guiding her.
CUT TO BLACK.
END OF EPISODE
